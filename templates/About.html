About Dementia Prediction


  • In This Project,We use 2 Algorithm:

  • Gradient boosting:Gradient boosted trees build the trees sequentially.
  • Random Forest:Ensemble method that combines multiple decision trees to improve the classification accuracy and reduce overfitting.

    • Dataset--- We pick dataset form Kaggle and our dataset name is CAD dementia dataset. Coloums

    • Visit - Number of visit
    • MR.Delay
    • Name - Patient name
    • M.F - Gender
    • Age - Patient age
    • EDUC - Years of education
    • Socioeconomic status as assessed by the Hollingshead Index of Social Position and classified into categories from 1 (highest status) to 5 (lowest status)

      • Clinical Info

    • MMSE - Mini-Mental State Examination score (range is from 0 = worst to 30 = best)
    • CDR - Clinical Dementia Rating (0 = no dementia, 0.5 = very mild AD, 1 = mild AD, 2 = moderate AD)

      • Derived anatomic volumes

    • eTIV - Estimated total intracranial volume, mm3
    • nWBV - Normalized whole-brain volume, expressed as a percent of all voxels in the atlas-masked image that are labeled as gray or white matter by the automated tissue segmentation process
    • ASF - Atlas scaling factor (unitless)
